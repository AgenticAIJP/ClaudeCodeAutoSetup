# How to use generate_test.txt (guide for humans)

A template for asking Claude to write tests.

## Why use a template

Ask just "write tests" and Claude tends to produce shallow
happy-path-only tests. Specifying edge cases and error handling
produces tests that actually catch bugs.

## How to use

1. Copy generate_test.txt
2. Write the target file/function after "Target:"
3. Paste into the chat

## Why tests matter extra in AI development

When you hand Claude a large change, existing tests mean
"if it breaks, you know immediately" — you can delegate safely.
Tests are the safety net of the AI era (AI TDD).
